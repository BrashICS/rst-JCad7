HANGMAN


How to play:

  • To start playing, chose a word topic from the list
  • Once chosen, click 'submit' and begin!
  • Once started, enter a guess (a letter) into the box titled 'Enter Letter', and click enter
  • If correct, it will add it to the underlined hidden word below, if incorrect it will add an image to the hangman graphic
  • If you get 6 guesses wrong, it will pop up an alert, saying you failed to guess, and when you click 'OK' it will refresh the page
  • If you know the word, enter your guess into the 'Final Guess' box, if you get it right it will pop up an alert saying you won, clicking 'OK' will refresh the page.
